About The Conclave

Empowering Future Roboticists

The All Kerala Robotics and Automation Society Conclave (AKRASC) is the premier flagship event organized by the IEEE RAS Kerala Chapter, meticulously designed for robotics enthusiasts, students, and professionals.

AKRASC serves as a collaborative platform where pioneering ideas meet technical expertise. Our goal is to host an exceptional experience, fostering knowledge-sharing, innovation, and networking in the field of robotics and automation across Kerala.

Venue Host

College of Engineering Trivandrum (CET)

Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala

Established in 1939, the College of Engineering Trivandrum (CET) is one of the most prestigious and oldest engineering institutions in India. Known for its academic excellence and research prowess, CET provides the perfect technological ecosystem to host AKRASC.

We are thrilled to announce that IEEE SB CET is taking the lead in organizing and hosting this exciting edition of the conclave, assuring top-notch execution and hospitality.
